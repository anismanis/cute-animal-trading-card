Sand Cat

A small wild cat.

  • Avg Weight: 2.8 kgs
  • Avg Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Dessert

The sand cat, also known as the sand dune cat, is a small wild cat living in sandy and stony deserts far from water sources. With its sandy to light grey fur, it is well camouflaged in a desert environment.
